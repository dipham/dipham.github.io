About Us

MForesight serves as the voice of the national advanced manufacturing community to the public and provides the federal government with intelligence about emerging technologies to shape priorities for future federal and private sector investment and set the trajectory of U.S. advanced manufacturing.

MForesight's vision
MForesight: Alliance for Manufacturing Foresight serves as a think-and-do tank to forecast next generation technologies that will lead to future U.S.-based manufacturing. MForesight will focus on providing intelligence to the U.S. government and the broader manufacturing community regarding emerging technologies and opportunities for public-private investments in advanced manufacturing. MForesight also seeks to promote technology innovation to bridge the gap between basic science and manufacturers.

MForesight’s mission:
  • Serve as a catalyst and a voice of America’s advanced manufacturing community
  • Engage experts from industry and academia to identify opportunities and challenges related to emerging technologies
  • Provide a continuous source of intelligence to federal science and technology agencies about challenges and opportunities in the manufacturing sector.
  • Align public and private research activities with important national priorities
  • Educate the public about prospects for advanced manufacturing
To innovate and to revitalize U.S. advanced manufacturing, we must ensure that the promising discoveries and inventions made by researchers in U.S. are matured and manufactured in the U.S., not left to chance or passively allowed to take root elsewhere.

Stakeholders for our work include federal agencies and congressional offices; industry; and academia and research labs to promote university/industry summer camps for middle and high school students, industry apprenticeships and internships, and focused programs in manufacturing in four-year colleges.

WHAT WE DO

MForesight achieves its goals through a variety of activities, including:

  • Convening national thought leaders to identify and prioritize nascent technologies;
  • Commissioning subject matter experts to assist in preparing timely reports on emerging technologies for future investments;
  • Serving as a national network for identifying and sharing best practices in technology commercialization and workforce development;
  • Communicating findings and articulating technology opportunities in advanced manufacturing to broader manufacturing community including NIST-MEPs;
  • Promoting career opportunities in engineering and advanced manufacturing with emphasis on encouraging participation of diverse workforce; and
  • Articulating criteria & methods for evaluation and course correction regarding technology forecasting.
  • These activities are carried out through assembling in-person workshops, talking to subject matter experts one-on-one, hosting virtual roundtables, writing reports[link], participating in conferences, and utilizing social media.

HISTORY

MForesight was established in October 2015 – the product of an NSF solicitation. The May 2015 solicitation was created upon recommendation by the President’s Council of Advisors on Science and Technology (“Report to the President: Accelerating U.S. Advanced Manufacturing” PDF), and its purpose was to establish a national consortium to provide coordinated private‐sector input on national advanced manufacturing technology research and development priorities. The University of Michigan team’s proposal was selected to carry out this important work.

At MForesight, we are all about connecting with others who have expertise and are interested in advanced manufacturing. If that describes you, we invite you to connect with us!
